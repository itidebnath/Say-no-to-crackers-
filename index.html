Why Crackers are Harmful?

Crackers contribute to extreme air pollution, noise pollution, and hazardous waste. This Diwali, we encourage everyone to avoid crackers and focus on sustainable ways to celebrate. Join the movement towards a cleaner and greener Diwali!
